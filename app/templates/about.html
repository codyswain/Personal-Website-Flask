About Me

This past summer I've spent my time interning at an SF-based tech startup called iBeat. iBeat has released a smart watch which uses cutting-edge sensors and artifical intelligence-based signal processing to detect life-threatening conditions in a user. I've spent the majority of my time creating data pipelines and building a database for the other data scientists to use. Additionally, I've spent a significant amount of time creating algorithms to extract features from watch sensor data, and I've analyzed this data using statistical analysis combined with machine-learning.

This upcoming Fall, I'll be transferring into my junior year at UCLA, to study computer science and engineering.
